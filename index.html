Building your squad

  • Add players one at a time with the number and name fields, or use Import from Excel to bring in a whole team sheet at once (works with headers like "Number"/"Name", or just two plain columns).
  • Every player you add sits in the Squad pool on the left until you place them on the pitch, the bench, or mark them unavailable.
  • Tap the ✕ on a squad chip to remove a player from the app completely.

Setting your line-up

  • Pick a formation from the dropdown above the pitch — the pitch positions update straight away.
  • Tap a player in the squad list to select them (you'll see a gold outline), then tap a pitch position to place them there.
  • To move a player who's already on the pitch, tap them once to select, then tap wherever you want them to go — another pitch spot, or a bench slot.
  • Tap the same already-selected player a second time (not on their number) to make them captain — you'll see a gold "C" badge appear.
  • Once a player is on the pitch, tap their number to edit it — a small input box appears, type the new number and press Enter.

Bench & unavailable players

  • The Bench works the same way as the pitch — select a player, then tap an empty bench slot.
  • For players who are injured, suspended, or missing, select them and tap the Unavailable box below the bench. They're pulled out of the pool so they can't be picked by accident.
  • Tap the ✕ on an unavailable player to bring them back into the squad pool.

Tactics Board

  • Switch to the Tactics Board tab for a free-form pitch with smaller player tokens, separate from your Match Day line-up.
  • Drag any token — or any player from the tray below — anywhere on the pitch to plan movement, pressing shape, or set-piece routines.
  • Load starting XI pulls in your current Match Day formation as a starting point. Reset to formation snaps back to that shape. Clear board empties it.
  • Drag a token off the pitch to send that player back to the tray.

Match details & sharing

  • Click the small gold text next to the club name at the top to set your team — e.g. "U12 Boys" or "U16 Girls" — so lineups are labelled for your squad.
  • Fill in the opponent, competition, date, and venue at the top — these are included when you save or share a lineup.
  • Use the Tactics Notes boxes for corner routines, set-pieces, and general reminders.
  • Save lineup stores everything — squad, formation, bench, unavailable list, tactics board, notes — so you can reload it later. Saved lineups appear in the list below the buttons.
  • Copy as text formats the whole line-up ready to paste into WhatsApp or email.
  • Print sheet gives you a clean printable version for the sideline.
  • Clear pitch empties the starting XI and bench only — your squad list and unavailable players stay put.
